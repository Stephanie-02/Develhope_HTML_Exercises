Buttermilk Pancake Recipe.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • Honey
  • Cooking oil
  • Sugar

Method

Step 1 Whisk together buttermilk,eggs and sugar.

Step 2 Whisk in oil and beat mixture to a thick smooth butter.

Step 3 Add flour into the mixture and beat until fully mixed.

Step 4 Pre-heat cooking pan and add a tsp of oil.Pour butter and spread evenly on pan.Cook each side until golden brown.

Serve


Dazzle honey/maple syrup on pancakes and serve them hot.

Tip


Tip: Be sure to preheat the oven to keep the pancakes warm while you cook the rest.It makes all the difference

Either

Instead of buttermilk,one could use yoghurt.Brings out even better results.
